Smart Hospitalization

The "Smart Hospitalization" web application allows you to observe and monitor the progress of vital patient parameters. It allows you to work on some environmental parameters to improve patient care. To start the application, click on the top left corner on "Smart Hospitalization Connected"

Patient

For each patient, information about vital parameters such as heart rate, oxygen pressure, and temperature can be displayed. It will be possible to actuate some instrument such as oxygen tank to change the patient's oxygen pressure. An auxiliary led will be used to notify that one of the vital parameters will be out of the safe values. To view the patient's card, click on "Patient" on the top-right menu.

Room

For each room, you can see information about the parameters such as room temperature and air conditioning level. It will be possible to actuate some instruments as I am conditioning to change the room temperature. An alarm will be used to notify that the temperature will be outside the safety values. To view the room's card, click on "Room" on the top-right menu.
